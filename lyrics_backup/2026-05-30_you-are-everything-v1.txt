[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Coffee cups and falling rain,
Washing out the windowpane.
But all the world is perfectly clear,
When I have got you sitting here.

[Verse 1]
A candle burning on the wood,
Making everything feel good.
Your hand is resting close to mine,
A perfect spark, a perfect sign.

[Pre-Chorus]
A quiet moment we can share,
With music hanging in the air.
The distance starts to fade away,
There is nothing left to say.

[Chorus]
You are everything that I desire,
A gentle spark that starts a fire.
A warm embrace when nights grow cold,
The greatest story I have ever told.

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (You are everything)
[Whispered] (You are everything)

[Verse 2]
The shadow falls across the room,
Sweeping out the midnight gloom.
Fingers tracing every line,
I am yours and you are mine.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The moonlight spills across the floor,
Leaving me just wanting more.
The distance finally melts away,
And I just want to make you stay.

[Chorus]
You are everything that I desire,
A gentle spark that starts a fire.
A warm embrace when nights grow cold,
The greatest story I have ever told.

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Echo] (You are everything)
[Whispered] (You are everything)

[Bridge]
Shadows dancing on the wall,
I am ready for the fall.
Love is all we really need,
Safe inside the arms of Saeed.
Lost within this quiet space,
Finding heaven in this place.

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I just want to hold you close forever.)
[Spoken] (You are my entire world.)

[Final Chorus]
You are everything that I desire,
A gentle spark that starts a fire.
A warm embrace when nights grow cold,
The greatest story I have ever told.
